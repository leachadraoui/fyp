Nestled inside the Crusader Castle, the Museum of the Archaeological Site of Byblos invites you on a journey through time. Every artifact, inscription, and object reveals part of the city's deep past ‚Äî from Neolithic tools to Phoenician sarcophagi and Roman remains.

The collection comes from decades of excavations carried out right on-site. Visitors can explore items displayed just steps away from where they were uncovered, creating a powerful connection to the land and its history.

Whether you're a traveler, a student, or simply curious, this museum offers a rare chance to connect with the origins of civilization and see where the alphabet was born.
