Vincent R.

Variety of must-see places

The citadel of Byblos is, in my opinion, one of the more beautiful places in Lebanon. There is such a variety of incredible and worth visiting places in the citadel, that you really need to see this and reserve some time if you visit Byblos. It's also really easy to visit if you are staying in Beirut.

VinnieLondon

Well worth the short trip from Beirut

Around one hour drive from Beirut depending on traffic the citadel an essential visit. Great historical site in and around the castle and lots of nice restaurants, bars and shops in a beautiful setting. Well worth the short trip from Beirut to spend a half day with lunch or dinner.

Chewy

Beautiful

Byblos is a must-visit UNESCO World Heritage site, offering a perfect mix of history, scenic views, and vibrant culture. This ancient city boasts well-preserved ruins dating back to 8000 BC and visitors can explore at their own pace. With multilingual historical signs and breathtaking scenery, it's an excellent spot for a perfect afternoon.
